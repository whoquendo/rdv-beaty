MASSAGES

It is the oldest therapeutic tool used by humans to provide a natural remedy against pain. Massage is a form of manipulation of the superficial and deep layers of the body's muscles using various techniques to improve their functions, aid in healing processes and to relax pain. There are no contraindications for massages, except for specific conditions that may prevent certain maneuvers, such as skin problems, etc.

    Some benefits to take into account when receiving a massage, are:

  • Pain relief.
  • Feeling of general well-being./li>
  • Relaxation of contracted muscles.
  • Long-term flexibility of tissues.
  • Improvement of blood and lymphatic circulation.

      • TYPES OF MASSAGE

  • Kobido: Japanese facial massage, natural flirting effect without surgery.
  • Decontracting: intense massage to relax muscles and relieve headaches, back, shoulders, arms and feet.
  • Relaxing: Medium pressure massage to relax your mind and pamper you.
  • Traditional Thai: massage clothed and on a futon on the floor. Stretching and acupressure.
